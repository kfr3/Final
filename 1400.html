Sandro Botticelli

❮ ❯

Sandro Botticelli, whose birth name is Alessandro di Mariano Filipepi, was born in the year 1445, and was another influential painter during the early Renaissance period in Italy. Studying underneath Filippo Lippi, who was a famous Florentine painter, Botticelli began to develop a style that would garner the attention of the Medici family, the most influential family of Italy at the time. As he did paintings for the family and around Europe, he began to build a reputation for his skill and ability to replicate nature in his paintings.
