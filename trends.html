Tranquility

With the world around us becoming more technologically advanced by the day, and people constantly rushing to make ends meet in this fast-paced society, people begin to yearn for a more peaceful and simple setting, where they can relax and not worry about interpretations and complexity. Calm art, with simple colors and patterns, is becoming more popular, as it is easy on the eyes and can invoke a calming effect when you see it, to give the viewer a break from the normally busy lives.
