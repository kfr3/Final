Jean-Michel Basquiat

❮ ❯

Jean-Michel Basquiat was born December 22nd, 1960 in New York City, United States. After being in a graffiti group, Basquiat's artwork began to receive attention from the art world, and he quickly became one of the most popular artists of the 1980's. Basquiat often involved themes surrounding race, poverty, and mentality into his work, and used it as a way to navigate his experience as a Black person in America and to criticize society as a whole.
